About Graduate Nurse/Midwife Job Match

The Graduate Nurse/Midwife Job Match initiative, spearheaded by the University of Southern Queensland and in collaboration with industry leaders, aims to revolutionize the way nursing and midwifery graduates connect with employment opportunities across Australia. Focused primarily on supporting roles in regional, rural, and remote areas, this platform is designed to streamline the job search process, offering tailored, relevant, and accessible information.

Our mission is driven by a deep understanding of the challenges faced by graduates in finding roles that align with their skills, preferences, and values. The platform not only facilitates job matching based on comprehensive criteria but also integrates essential tools and resources to empower graduates in making informed decisions.

Our Vision

At the heart of the Graduate Nurse/Midwife Job Match platform is a vision to create a seamless, intuitive, and empowering experience for graduates, helping them to find their 'best fit' positions. By leveraging advanced technologies, including GIS integration and sophisticated filtering mechanisms, we are committed to providing a platform where graduates can effectively showcase their profiles, explore diverse opportunities, and connect with potential employers across Australia.

Meet Our Team

The project is guided by a team of dedicated professionals from UniSQ and partner institutions, all committed to enhancing graduate employment outcomes in the nursing and midwifery sectors. Associate Professor Dr. Lisa Beccaria, along with Dr. Helen Donovan from QUT, leads our interdisciplinary team, bringing together expertise in healthcare, technology, and education to deliver a solution that meets the needs of graduates and employers alike.
